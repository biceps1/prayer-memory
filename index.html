Introduction

Prayer Memory ("the app") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our iPhone and Apple Watch application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Prayer Memory is designed to function entirely on your devices:

  • No personal data is collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking are used
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No identifiers such as name, email, or location are collected

Local Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your iPhone and Apple Watch using Apple's secure on-device storage mechanisms.

This includes:

  • Prayer calibration timings
  • Prayer session history
  • Rakaa and tasbeeh counts
  • App preferences such as language settings

This data never leaves your devices and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Device Synchronization

Prayer Memory may sync data between your iPhone and Apple Watch using Apple's secure Watch Connectivity framework. This synchronization:

  • Occurs directly between your paired devices
  • Uses Apple's encrypted communication protocols
  • Does not involve any external servers
  • Keeps all data within your personal device ecosystem

Third-Party Services

Prayer Memory does not use:

  • Third-party analytics services
  • Advertising frameworks
  • Tracking technologies
  • External data services

User Feedback & Reviews

The app may display Apple's native in-app review prompt using official system APIs. Any reviews or ratings are handled entirely by Apple and are not accessible to us.

Children's Privacy

Prayer Memory does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Data Deletion

You can delete all locally stored data at any time by:

  • Removing the app from your iPhone
  • Removing the app from your Apple Watch
  • Using the reset option within the app settings (if available)

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
